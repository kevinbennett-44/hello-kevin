 Playbook 

Research. Define. Ideate. Test. Iterate. Repeat.

One thing they all have in common though, is that the tools and processes they use, in the way they use them, is unique to each designer or organisation.

In that vain, I wanted to document how I approach the design process ‚Äì the steps I take and the tools and methods I use to try to find the best solutions for product and design projects.
